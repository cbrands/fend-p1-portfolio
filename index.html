Famous items in The Netherlands

Tulips

Tulips are delightful flowers available in many colors. Tulips bloom in April and May. If youlike flowers be sure to visit the Keukenhof in april or May. Keukenhof is a park where more than 7 million flower bulbs are planted every year. More information.

Windmills

Windmills are possibly the most well known symbol for the Netherlands. Holland still has over 1,000 historic vertical mills, more than any other country. More information.

Wooden shoes (Clogs)

Clogs have been traditional footwear for hundreds of years. Some people think that the Dutch still wear clogs every day. But you are not likely to see them on the streets. More information.
